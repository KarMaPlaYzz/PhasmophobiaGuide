## 1. Introduction

PhasmophobiaGuide ("App", "we", "us", or "our") operates the PhasmophobiaGuide mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection when you use our App.

This Privacy Policy applies to our App and explains our data practices. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account & Authentication:**
- We do not require user accounts or authentication
- No personal information (name, email, phone number) is collected
- No login credentials are stored

**In-App Purchases:**
- When you purchase premium features via in-app purchase, transaction data is handled by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
- We store locally on your device:
  - Premium status (yes/no)
  - Purchase date
  - These are stored in encrypted local storage (AsyncStorage) on your device only

**Bookmarks & Custom Data:**
- Ghost bookmarks, equipment presets, and notes you create are stored locally on your device
- This data is not sent to our servers
- You can export or delete this data anytime through the app settings

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Advertising Data:**
- **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob):** When ads are displayed, Google collects:
  - Ad impressions and interactions
  - Approximate location (based on IP)
  - Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  - App usage data related to ad serving
- **Ad Revenue:** We receive aggregate (non-personal) ad revenue data

**App Analytics:**
- We do not currently use Firebase Analytics or third-party analytics
- Crash reporting: Not currently implemented (coming soon)

**Device Information:**
- Device type and OS version (for app compatibility)
- Platform (iOS or Android)
- This is used to ensure the app functions properly

**Logs:**
- Console logs for debugging (not sent anywhere)
- Stored locally on your device only
